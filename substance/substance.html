SUBSTANCE

A unique ecosystem of tools and content dedicated to materials

  • EMPOWERING THE ARTIST IN YOU

    Paint and texture 3D models faster than ever.
    Create complex masks and effects in seconds.

  • AN EVER-GROWING LIBRARY

    All materials are crafted in-house or
    by world renowned artists, so quality is consistently of the highest standard.

  • SUBSTANCE MATERIALS

    Substance materials contain a set of digital textures that can be driven by parameters,
    allowing for an infinity of variations from a single file. Substance materialss are agnostic
    and are compatible with all 3D tools, engines and renderers on the market.

  • ANY AND EVERY MATERIAL

    From real life finishes to stylized surfaces -
    Scanned, procedural or hybrid materials.
